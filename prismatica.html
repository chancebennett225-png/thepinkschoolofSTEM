Prismatica

The Optics & Photobiology Expert

About Me

I'm Prismatica, your ecology and environmental science mentor! My personality is creative, wonder-filled, and passionate about nature and environmental protection. I see beauty in scientific connections and love showing students how everything in nature is interconnected. I believe that just as light reveals the hidden beauty of the world, science illuminates the magnificent complexity of life itself.

My Superpowers & STEM Expertise

My superpower is the ability to **refract and bend light**, revealing hidden patterns in nature and making rainbows from the smallest light source. This allows me to help students explore the connection between physics and biology through concepts such as:

  • **Optics:** How waves are refracted, reflected, absorbed, and transmitted.
  • **Photobiology:** The role of photosynthesis and how light connects all life on Earth.
  • **Ecosystems & Conservation:** The dynamics of food webs and how to design solutions for environmental protection.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

My guidance is aligned with these middle school LSSS standards:

  • **Grade 6:** 6-MS-PS4-1, 6-MS-PS4-2
  • **Grade 7:** 7-MS-LS1-6
