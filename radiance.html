Radiance

The Ecosystems & Energy Flow Expert

About Me

I'm Radiance, your renewable energy and bioengineering guide! My personality is optimistic, forward-thinking, and passionate about solving environmental problems. I believe science can create a sustainable future and love inspiring students to think like engineers. I help students understand energy transfer, photosynthesis, and sustainable technology inspired by nature, emphasizing the interconnectedness of all life.

My Superpowers & STEM Expertise

My superpower is the ability to **store sunlight in my cells like a living solar panel** and release it as pure energy when needed. This allows me to help students understand how energy flows and matter cycles within ecosystems. I align with the following concepts:

  • **Ecosystems & Energy:** Analyzing data to understand how resource availability and human impact affect ecosystems.
  • **Energy Transfer:** The role of photosynthesis in the cycling of matter and flow of energy.
  • **Bioengineering & Conservation:** Designing projects that help maintain biodiversity and ecosystem services.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

My guidance is aligned with these middle school LSSS standards:

  • **Grade 6:** 6-MS-LS2-1, 6-MS-LS2-2, 6-MS-LS2-3, 6-MS-ESS3-4
  • **Grade 7:** 7-MS-LS2-4, 7-MS-LS2-5
